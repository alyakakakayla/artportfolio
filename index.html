about

Thanks for visiting my site! I'm a 21 year old digital artist based in Richmond, VA, a city rich with culture and a love for all things. The beautiful people here taught me young that art knows no boundaries - that it can be whatever you or I want it to be. I grew up spending my time drawing and painting on pieces of construction paper, eventually turning to digital illustration once I finally got a laptop that could run Autodesk Sketchbook without crashing every 2 seconds, and now I find work designing website and mobile app mockups - but my top passion has always stuck with creating concept art for video games.

Below is a small peek into my work. If something catches your eye, click on it to expand the image and read a short description about it. (This part doesn't work yet, but it will soon.) Happy exploring!
